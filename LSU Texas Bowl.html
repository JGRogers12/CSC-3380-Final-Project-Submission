Dear LSU Students,

We are excited to announce that LSU has accepted an invitation to play in the TaxAct Texas Bowl vs. Kansas State on January 4th at 8:00 p.m. CT at NRG Stadium in Houston, TX.

Thank you for showing your support for LSU football this season. You can purchase tickets to the Texas Bowl here.

All tickets purchased through our office will be $100. Click here to see a seating chart of NRG Stadium. 

Students have until 5pm on Wednesday, December 8th for priority seating in the student section. If you are part of a group that wants to sit together, please place the names of the other students you would like to sit with in the comments box.

All tickets will be sent via mobile delivery at a later date.

For information on NRG Stadium including seating charts, parking, and other gameday policies and procedures, please visit TaxActTexasBowl.com.

Please visit Bowl Central at LSUsports.net for more bowl game information.

Thank you again for purchasing bowl tickets to see the Tigers play and proving once again Tiger fans are truly the best fans in America!
